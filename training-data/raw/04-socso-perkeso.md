# SOCSO (PERKESO) Contribution Rates - 2025
# 社会保险 (SOCSO/PERKESO) 缴纳率 - 2025
# Kadar Caruman PERKESO - 2025
Source: perkeso.gov.my
Keywords: 社保 社会保险 SOCSO PERKESO EIS SIP 工伤 失业保险 雇主 雇员 caruman insurans pekerjaan

## Coverage
- Employees' Social Security Act 1969 (Act 4)
- Employment Insurance System Act 2017 (Act 800 / EIS/SIP)

## Wage Ceiling
- Effective 1 October 2024: RM6,000/month (increased from RM5,000)
- Employees earning above RM6,000 are assessed using RM6,000 ceiling

## SOCSO (Act 4) Contribution Rates

### First Category (Invalidity + Employment Injury)
| Party | Rate |
|---|---|
| Employer | 1.75% of insured wages |
| Employee | 0.5% of insured wages |
| Total | 2.25% |

### Second Category (Employment Injury Only)
- For employees aged 60 and above
| Party | Rate |
|---|---|
| Employer | 1.25% of insured wages |
| Employee | 0% |
| Total | 1.25% |

## EIS / SIP (Act 800) Contribution Rates
| Party | Rate |
|---|---|
| Employer | 0.2% of insured wages |
| Employee | 0.2% of insured wages |
| Total | 0.4% |

## Eligibility
### SOCSO (Act 4)
- All employees with monthly wages ≤ RM6,000 (mandatory)
- Employees with wages > RM6,000 at time of first contribution: optional
- Malaysian citizens and Permanent Residents only
- Employees below 60 years old: First Category
- Employees 60 and above: Second Category (employment injury only)

### EIS (Act 800)
- Malaysian citizens and Permanent Residents only
- Employees aged 18 to 60
- Domestic workers, self-employed, civil servants, and statutory body employees are excluded

## Benefits Covered

### SOCSO Benefits
1. Medical benefits for employment injury
2. Temporary disablement benefit
3. Permanent disablement benefit
4. Dependants' benefit (death due to employment injury)
5. Funeral benefit
6. Invalidity pension
7. Invalidity grant
8. Constant attendance allowance
9. Rehabilitation facilities

### EIS Benefits
1. Job Search Allowance (JSA) — up to 6 months
2. Early Re-employment Allowance
3. Reduced Income Allowance
4. Training Allowance
5. Training Fee

## Payment Deadline
- Contributions must be paid by the 15th of the following month
- Late payment: RM10,000 fine or 2 years imprisonment or both

## Foreign Workers
- SOCSO coverage available under separate schedule (Third Schedule, Act 4)
- Different contribution rates apply
- EIS does NOT cover foreign workers
