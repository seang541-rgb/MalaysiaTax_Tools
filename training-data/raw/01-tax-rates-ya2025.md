## Resident Individual Tax Rates (YA 2023, 2024 & 2025)

| Chargeable Income (RM) | Tax Rate | Cumulative Tax (RM) |
|---|---|---|
| 0 - 5,000 | 0% | 0 |
| 5,001 - 20,000 | 1% | 150 |
| 20,001 - 35,000 | 3% | 600 |
| 35,001 - 50,000 | 6% | 1,500 |
| 50,001 - 70,000 | 11% | 3,700 |
| 70,001 - 100,000 | 19% | 9,400 |
| 100,001 - 400,000 | 25% | 84,400 |
| 400,001 - 600,000 | 26% | 136,400 |
| 600,001 - 2,000,000 | 28% | 528,400 |
| Above 2,000,000 | 30% | - |

## Non-Resident Tax Rate
Flat rate of 30% on all chargeable income (no progressive bands).

## Tax Rebates
- Personal rebate: RM400 if chargeable income ≤ RM35,000
- Spouse rebate: Additional RM400 if spouse has no income and chargeable income ≤ RM35,000
- Zakat/Fitrah: Deducted from tax payable (not from income)
