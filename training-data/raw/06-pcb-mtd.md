# PCB (Potongan Cukai Bulanan / Monthly Tax Deduction)
Source: LHDN Malaysia

## What is PCB?
PCB (Potongan Cukai Bulanan) or MTD (Monthly Tax Deduction) is the mechanism where employers deduct income tax from employees' monthly salary as advance tax payment to LHDN.

## Legal Basis
- Income Tax (Deduction from Remuneration) Rules 1994
- Employers are legally required to make PCB deductions

## Who is Subject to PCB?
- All employees receiving employment income in Malaysia
- Includes salary, wages, bonus, commission, allowances, benefits-in-kind
- Both residents and non-residents

## How PCB is Calculated
PCB is calculated based on:
1. Monthly gross remuneration
2. Employee's tax residency status
3. Marital status (single/married)
4. Number of qualifying children
5. EPF contributions (employee portion)
6. Zakat payments
7. Approved deductions (TP1 form claims)

### Simplified PCB Formula
1. Annualize monthly income: Monthly × 12 = Annual
2. Deduct EPF (employee share): Annual × 11% (capped at RM4,000 relief)
3. Deduct personal relief: RM9,000
4. Deduct spouse relief (if applicable): RM4,000
5. Deduct child relief: RM2,000 × number of children
6. Apply progressive tax rates to get annual tax
7. Monthly PCB = Annual tax ÷ 12

## TP1 and TP3 Forms
### TP1 Form (Employee)
- Employee declares additional reliefs/deductions to employer
- Allows employer to reduce PCB deduction
- Must be submitted to employer by end of the year

### TP3 Form (Employer)
- Employer advises LHDN of employee's PCB details
- Used when employee has multiple employers

## Payment by Employer
- PCB must be remitted to LHDN by the 15th of the following month
- Via e-PCB system (e-cp39.hasil.gov.my)
- Penalty for late payment: 10% of unpaid amount

## Year-End Reconciliation
- Total PCB paid during the year is compared with actual tax liability
- If PCB > actual tax: Employee gets a refund
- If PCB < actual tax: Employee pays the difference
- Employees must file annual tax return (Form BE) even if PCB is deducted

## Bonus & Additional Payments
- PCB on bonus is calculated using the net PCB method
- The bonus is added to the annual income projection
- PCB for the bonus month will be higher

## CP38 (Additional Deduction)
- LHDN may instruct employer to make additional deductions
- Used when employee has outstanding tax arrears
- Employer must comply with CP38 instruction

## Key Changes (Recent)
- e-PCB is now mandatory for all employers
- LHDN calculator tool available at: https://e-cp39.hasil.gov.my/
